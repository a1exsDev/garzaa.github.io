Adrian Garza

Hello.

I'm a senior studying AI and machine learning at Columbia University. In my free time I code, write, and make games and artwork.

In summer 2017 I worked at Autodesk and built a client-facing GUI and some monitoring tools for their cloud deployment pipeline.

Apps

Fridge

Winner of People's Choice at the 2017 Code2040 Fellows Hackathon, Fridge aims to connect charities and food banks with companies and private events that end up with extra food.

Streaker

Streaker lets you build personal momentum by logging a task repeated each day. Build up a streak, but don't skip a day or you'll lose it.

mPlayer

A compact, lightweight (yet still full-featured) music player for when I want to make a mixtape for a friend, but the obscure mix of songs has to be collected from Spotify, Bandcamp, and Soundcloud.

D&D

An all-in-one Dungeons and Dragons dice roller/item generator. A lot of the data was gathered from /r/BehindTheTables, but a good deal of it is my own creation.

Terminal

An html/css/js terminal emulator. I made it in a day for a coding challenge and have since expanded upon it by adding various small, helpful functions.

Games

Valentine-404

A vaporwave-themed metroidvania. Open source and still in development. I'm currently finishing up the movement and combat system so I can do more meta-administrative functions like level transitions, game saving, and inventory. Check the source code here.

/re/

My final project for my reincarnation class: a simple platformer where your past bodies stay in the level and need to be used to get past hazards and over gaps. Play it here.

BaristaSim

Create endless, diverse coffee orders for a hungry, pumpkin-esque humanoid. There's a story in the works (much like the bartending game VA-11 Hall-A) but for now, enjoy the faithful representation of making an endless stream of drinks for one volatile customer.

Morrow Peak

A project that was never fully realized, Morrow Peak boasts an inventory system, full-featured NPC interactions, a hybrid 2D/2.5D system, and some of my finest pixel art and character animations. It also has three levels in total.
